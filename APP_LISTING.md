## Full Description (2800 chars max)

Shopify discontinued Stocky — StockPilot picks up where it left off.

StockPilot gives you everything you need to manage inventory, suppliers, and purchase orders without spreadsheets or expensive ERPs. Built specifically for Shopify merchants who need a simple, affordable way to stay on top of their stock.

**PURCHASE ORDERS**
Create professional purchase orders in seconds. Add items manually or use Fill Shelves to auto-calculate what you need based on sales velocity. Email POs directly to suppliers or download as PDF. Track status from draft to received.

**SUPPLIER MANAGEMENT**
Keep all your supplier details in one place — contacts, lead times, payment terms, currencies. Link products to suppliers so reordering is one click away. No more digging through emails or spreadsheets.

**FILL SHELVES — SMART REORDERING**
The feature Stocky merchants loved most. StockPilot analyzes your sales velocity and tells you exactly what to order and how much. Choose 14, 30, 60, or 90 days of coverage. Never run out of your best sellers again.

**RECEIVING WITH SHOPIFY SYNC**
Receive inventory against purchase orders — full or partial. StockPilot automatically updates your Shopify inventory levels and unit costs. No double-entry, no mistakes.

**INVENTORY COUNTS (STOCKTAKES)**
Run full or partial inventory counts. Enter actual quantities, review variances, and apply adjustments to Shopify with one click. Know exactly where your stock numbers don't match reality.

**DEMAND FORECASTING**
See which products need reordering before you run out. StockPilot calculates reorder points based on sales velocity, supplier lead times, and safety stock buffers. Spot rising and falling trends early.

**REPORTS THAT MATTER**
- ABC Analysis: Know which products drive 80% of your revenue
- Low Stock Alerts: Items with less than 14 days of stock
- Dead Stock Report: Products with zero sales in 90 days — and their dollar value sitting on your shelves
- Weekly email digest with key inventory metrics

**MULTI-LOCATION TRANSFERS**
Got multiple locations? StockPilot suggests transfers from overstocked locations to understocked ones based on sales velocity at each location.

**PRICE LABELS**
Print price labels from received purchase orders. Download as PDF, print, and tag your products.

**BUILT FOR STOCKY MERCHANTS**
If you used Stocky and need a replacement before August 2026, StockPilot was built for you. Same core features, modern interface, works on all Shopify plans.
